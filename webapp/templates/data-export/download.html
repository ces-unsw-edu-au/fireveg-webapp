File download

We have prepared template workbooks to summarise the fire ecology traits for plant species from the information of the database. This is a snapshot of the database in its current state of development, and entries are still subject to data curation and quality assessment, DATA IS NOT READY FOR FINAL USE OR CRITICAL APPLICATIONS AND YOU SHOULD NOT DISTRIBUTE THIS DATA.

Please cite this work as: Ferrer-Paris, J. R. and Keith, D. A. (2022) Fire Ecology Traits for Plants: A database for fire research and management. Version 1.00. Centre for Ecosystem Science, University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia.

Data from field forms

The file is a Excel Workbook (XLSX format), output has been tested in Excel desktop app and Excel for the web, but might not work in other office software.

Field sites information is summarised in the Sites tab (green tab). Species records in each sample are in the Samples tab (green tab). There is also a tab with a list of species.
